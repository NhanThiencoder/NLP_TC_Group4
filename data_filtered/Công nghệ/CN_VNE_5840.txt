Lỗi trong Excel do virus? (2)

Toi dang su dung OS WIN XP SP2. OFFICE XP. Khi mo Excel len thi hien ra bang bao co 3 button o duoi: 

1. Disable macros 

2. Enable macros 

3. More Info

Phai bam vao button thu 1, sau do hien ra 1 bang thong bao nua. Toi bam YES thi du lieu Excel moi xuat hien tren man hinh. Co phai may tinh cua toi bi nhiem virus khong? va do la virus gi? Xin giup toi cach khac phuc hien tuong tren. Cam on rat nhieu!
